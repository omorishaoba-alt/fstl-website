# First Standard Title Limited (FSTL)

**Verification • Intelligence • Trust**

FSTL is a mobile-first, cloud-native verification and intelligence company.
We operate without physical infrastructure, leveraging banking rails, AI,
and distributed agents to deliver trusted verification services at scale.

## What We Do
- Digital verification of titles, records, and references
- Bank-backed receipts and confirmations
- AI-powered intelligence search
- Agent-driven field verification

## How It Works
Input → Transaction → Output

Clients initiate verification.
Payments are made directly to FSTL’s bank.
Verification is completed digitally and confirmed with receipts.

## Strategic Advantage
- Zero physical infrastructure
- Bank-centered financial processing
- AI-powered search and intelligence
- Operates quietly, scales rapidly
