Welcome to FSTL OS

For over 20 years, First Standard Title Limited has been pioneering trust, verification, and investment infrastructure. Our mission is to elevate individuals out of poverty by transforming real assets into investment-grade capital.

FSTL OS is a fully digital operating system, backed by AI, that allows individuals, agents, and institutions to convert assets into capital, safely and securely.

What is FSTL OS?

FSTL OS is a digital operating system that manages verification, asset transformation, and capital creation. Just like Windows, macOS, or Linux, it provides a platform—but specific to trust, verification, and financial collateralization in underdeveloped economies.

Unlike traditional land registries (GIS) or C of O systems:

  • GIS provides maps and basic property data but cannot transform assets into capital.
  • C of O verifies ownership but does not enable financial leverage or liquidity.
  • FSTL OS does both: verifies ownership and transforms verified assets into capital ready for investment or loans.

FAQ

What is FSTL OS?

It is a digital operating system for asset verification, collateralization, and capital creation, globally applicable.

How does it differ from GIS or C of O?

GIS maps and C of O registers property. FSTL OS verifies, transforms, and enables financial leverage.

Who can use FSTL OS?

Individuals, agents, investment representatives, and institutional clients with real assets can all use it.

What ID is required?

Individuals: NIN and BVN. Agents/representatives: CAC registration if applicable. Institutions: Extra due diligence based on asset size.
