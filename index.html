About FSTL OS

For over 20 years, First Standard Title Limited has studied the challenges of turning real assets into verified, usable capital. The FSTL OS provides a digital, globally applicable solution that allows individuals, agents, investment representatives, and institutions to verify assets, create collateral, and access investment opportunities efficiently and securely.

Our Vision

To elevate people out of poverty by transforming real assets into verifiable, investment-ready capital worldwide.

Our Mission

To provide a fully digital verification, title insurance, and investment infrastructure that is accessible, transparent, and auditable.

Our Objectives

  • Enable individuals to verify assets and unlock capital immediately.
  • Provide agents and investment representatives with structured earning opportunities.
  • Support institutions with reliable, auditable, and verifiable assets for financial products.

How FSTL OS Works

  1. Verify Your Assets: Enter your NIN, BVN, or property information.
  2. Generate Verified Collateral: Receive PDF and QR code proving your asset is clean and ready for collateralization.
  3. Access Investment Opportunities: Use verified collateral to apply for loans or investment products.
  4. Protect Yourself and Institutions: Buy title or mortgage insurance.
  5. Engage Agents or Investment Representatives: Join our program and earn commissions while assisting clients.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a bank account to use FSTL OS?
No, but providing BVN helps speed verification and integration with financial services.
Can institutions use the OS for large-scale assets?
Yes, with additional due diligence depending on asset size.
Are agents or investment representatives required to have business registration?
Yes, CAC registration or business license is required for corporate representatives.
Is my verification secure?
Yes. Data is encrypted, and verification results are tamper-proof, backed by AI.
