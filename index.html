Frequently Asked Questions

What is FSTL OS?

FSTL OS is Nigeria’s first open operating system for verification, investment, and trust infrastructure. It operates like a digital OS, similar to Windows or Android, but specific to asset verification and collateralization.

Why should I use FSTL OS instead of GIS or C of O?

Unlike GIS or C of O, FSTL OS allows you to transform real assets into verified collateral, create capital, and directly access loans or investment opportunities. It’s fast, transparent, and bank-backed.

Who can use FSTL OS?

Retail users, agents, investment representatives, institutions, and government bodies can all use the OS depending on their role and needs.

How does verification work?

Assets are submitted via forms → validated and verified by FSTL OS → secure verification code generated → output is usable for loans, insurance, or investment.
