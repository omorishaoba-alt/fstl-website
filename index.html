FSTL OS Flow

Like Windows, macOS, or Linux, FSTL OS is an operating system. But it is specific: it manages asset verification, collateralisation, and capital creation digitally.

Input: Raw assets or documents
Transformation: FSTL verification, PDF issuance, QR generation, and AI validation
Output: Verified collateral, investment-grade documents, and actionable capital
