Frequently Asked Questions

Who can use FSTL OS?

Individuals, agents, investment representatives, and institutions. Retail and agents require NIN/BVN; additional verification for businesses and institutions.

Why use FSTL OS over GIS or C of O?

FSTL OS converts assets into verified collateral for capital generation. GIS and C of O only provide property data; they cannot generate investment-ready capital.

How do I generate verified documents?

Use the PDF/QR generation tools in the OS, backed by a secure verification database and AI search, without exposing API keys publicly.
