Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How is FSTL OS different from GIS?

A: GIS maps land but does not verify ownership or generate bank-ready collateral. FSTL OS converts assets into actionable financial instruments.

Q: How is FSTL OS different from C of O?

A: A C of O proves ownership but cannot be used directly to create capital. FSTL OS verification + collateralization enables immediate financial use.

Q: Can anyone use FSTL OS?

A: Yes. Individuals can use retail services, and institutions can submit portfolios for investment-grade verification.
