Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: What is FSTL OS?

A digital operating system for verification, investment, and trust infrastructure. It enables individuals and institutions to convert verified assets into capital.

Q2: How does it differ from GIS or C of O?

Unlike GIS or C of O, FSTL OS provides real-time verification, AI-backed search, PDF/QR receipts, and a pathway to convert verified assets into investment-grade capital.

Q3: How do retail users benefit?

Convert verified assets into loans, insurance, or collateral-backed investments, with minimal cost.

Q4: How do institutions benefit?

Gain trustworthy verification, transparent digital receipts, and access to securitized, investment-grade assets.

Q5: How are commissions handled?

Automatically via our cloud-powered wallet system; agents and representatives receive instant, verified payouts.
