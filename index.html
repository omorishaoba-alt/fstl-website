Our Journey and Mission

With over 20 years of experience, FSTL OS has been built to transform real assets into verified collateral, making financial access seamless. Our mission is to elevate individuals out of poverty by unlocking the latent value of assets in underdeveloped economies. The FSTL OS is a digital operating system designed to facilitate verification, title insurance, and investment-grade capital generation.

Retail Users

Affordable Access

Individuals can use FSTL products and services directly at a flat rate of 50,000 Naira to verify assets and create capital.

Collateralization

Verified assets can be used as clean, bankable collateral for loans, investments, and inheritance planning.

Ease of Use

Simple processes designed for articulate and inarticulate users alike, either directly or via agents at reasonable rates.

Institutional Users

Asset Transformation

Financial institutions and governments can convert verified assets into investment-grade capital such as ABS, MBS, corporate bonds, and municipal bonds.

Confidence & Security

All verifications are conducted with the highest security standards, ensuring trust and regulatory compliance.

Cost Effective

Institutional rate is 5% of the asset value, providing scalable access to asset-backed capital creation.

Investment Representatives

Representatives are authorized to use FSTL branded business cards and earn 20% commission on referred clients, while agents earn 10%. This program ensures wide adoption while maintaining brand integrity.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is FSTL OS?

A digital operating system that verifies assets, provides title and mortgage insurance, and converts them into bankable, investable collateral.

Who can use FSTL?

Individuals, agents, investment representatives, and institutions worldwide, starting with Nigeria as a pilot.

How do I generate verified collateral?

Use our online AI search, PDF, and QR tools securely connected to the backend verification database.

How much does it cost?

Retail users: 50,000 Naira flat rate. Institutions: 5% of asset value.
