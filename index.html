Welcome to FSTL OS

With over 20 years of experience, First Standard Title Limited (FSTL) has developed a digital operating system (OS) that transforms real-world assets into investment-grade collateral, creating capital for individuals, businesses, and institutions worldwide.

Our mission is to elevate people out of poverty by unlocking the latent value in tangible assets. Unlike traditional GIS or C of O systems, FSTL OS delivers immediate, verifiable, and actionable collateralization.

FSTL OS is backed by AI and is designed for global scalability, starting with Nigeria as a pilot.

Retail Users

FSTL empowers individuals to use their assets to generate capital and access loans directly or through agents.

How it works:

  1. Input your asset information via our verification forms.
  2. Transformation through FSTL OS verification and collateralization.
  3. Output: verified capital-ready documents, PDF/QR-enabled, ready for financial institutions.

Pricing: Flat rate of ₦50,000 for retail verification.

Institutions

For banks, investors, and government entities, FSTL OS converts assets into investment-grade instruments such as ABS, MBS, corporate bonds, and municipal bonds.

How it works:

  1. Institutions submit asset data.
  2. FSTL OS validates and verifies, generating PDF/QR-based verification.
  3. Output: Capital-ready, legally recognized collateral documents.

Pricing: 5% of asset value.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How is FSTL OS different from GIS or C of O?

A: GIS maps land; C of O grants ownership. Neither converts assets into verified collateral for capital creation. FSTL OS verifies, collateralizes, and creates actionable investment-ready assets.

Q: Who can use FSTL OS?

A: Retail users, agents, investment representatives, institutions, and government entities can all use FSTL OS according to their needs.

Q: How do I start generating capital?

A: Submit asset details, generate verified PDF/QR documents, and present them to financial institutions for loans or investment.
