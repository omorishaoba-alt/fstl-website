FAQ – FSTL Operating System

What is FSTL?

FSTL is a full digital OS for verification, title insurance, and capitalisation. It turns assets into bank-recognized collateral instantly.

How is FSTL different from GIS or C of O?

FSTL provides actionable verification, generates financial leverage, and issues immediate digital receipts. GIS and C of O are reference-only.

How do I use FSTL as a retail user?

Submit your asset or title document. Pay the flat-rate verification fee. Receive a bank-recognized PDF receipt and QR code.
